About the Company

SnackMix Vending blends three snack universes in one machine — Healthy (nuts, protein bars), Classic (chips, chocolate, sodas) and Exotic (Asian & Latin treats). We place machines in high-traffic spots like universities and business centers, keep the assortment fresh, and keep prices fair.

Our mission is to make snacking fun, varied and accessible 24/7. We partner with local distributors and importers to bring limited items each month.

  • Curated three-zone layout for easy choice.
  • Contactless payment & bright UI on the machine.
  • Monthly "Snack of the Month" — exotic limited drops.

Location
