About the Company

SnackMix Vending brings three snack universes into a single, user-friendly machine: Healthy (nuts, protein bars), Classic (chips, chocolate, sodas) and Exotic (Asian & Latin treats). We place machines where people need quick, quality snacks — campuses, offices, and public spaces. Our focus is variety, fair pricing and monthly product drops to keep customers coming back.

  • Curated 3-zone layout for easy choice and discovery.
  • Contactless payment and modern UI on the machine.
  • Seasonal & limited items — Snack of the Month program.
